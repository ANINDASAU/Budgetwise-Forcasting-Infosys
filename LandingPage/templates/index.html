About


BudgetWise is an AI-powered expense forecasting tool that helps individuals and businesses manage their finances more effectively. By analyzing past spending patterns with machine learning models such as ARIMA, Prophet, Random Forest, and LSTM, it provides accurate predictions of future expenses. The platform offers a simple interface to record expenses, view summaries, and explore visual charts for better insights. With smart alerts and adaptive learning, BudgetWise makes financial planning easier, helping users avoid overspending and build better saving habits.
